Which Pixel 10 model should you buy?

Buy Pixel 10 if you want the best value

Pixel 10 gives you a 6.3-inch Actua display (60–120 Hz), Tensor G5, 12 GB RAM, and a 4,970 mAh typical battery — usually the best-value pick.

Buy Pixel 10 Pro if you want the best camera experience

Pixel 10 Pro upgrades you to a Super Actua LTPO display (1–120 Hz), 16 GB RAM, and the Pro triple rear camera system with Pro Res Zoom up to 100×.

Buy Pixel 10 Pro XL if you want the biggest screen and battery

Pixel 10 Pro XL keeps Pro features, adds the largest 6.8-inch display, a 5,200 mAh typical battery, and faster Pixelsnap wireless charging (up to 25 W).

FAQs

Is Pixel 10 Pro XL worth it over Pixel 10 Pro?

Choose Pro XL if you want the bigger 6.8-inch display, larger battery, and higher Pixelsnap wireless charging rate. Choose Pro if you prefer the 6.3-inch size.

Do all Pixel 10 models use the same chip?

Yes — Pixel 10, Pixel 10 Pro and Pixel 10 Pro XL list Google Tensor G5 and Titan M2 security coprocessor.

How long do Pixel 10 phones get updates?

Google lists seven years of OS, security and Pixel Drop updates, and these phones launch with Android 16.
